My name's Steve Rokitka (Rocketkar) - I'm a designer, writer and artist, and the principal force behind Spinetree Media. I've been making things with words and images for most of my life, and doing it professionally for the better part of a decade. In that time I've designed a top-selling iOS app, created numerous websites and web assets, and designed enough t-shirts that they account for roughly half my wardrobe.

I spent the last several years as UI/UX lead at a startup, building a complex generative music app and all the assets, marketing materials, websites, and promo schwag that goes along with it. Once the app was released, I decided to take some time off and cross a few (okay, most) of the items off my bucket list. I painted a house, bought a motorcycle and learned to ride, took a Wilderness First Responder course, spent two quarters learning to fix cars at a community college, and continued my training in Krav Maga.

Taking a break from design was really incredible in that it helped deprogram me from a lot of the things that were holding me back professionally. From working with cars I learned to find something to measure to determine whether a job was successful. From training Krav Maga I learned how even a master continues to train and retrain their fundamentals. From getting first responder certfication I learned how a professional doesn't show up to a situation and take on that situation's energy - they show up and set the tone for how a problem is going to get solved.

Today, I'm back at work as a freelancer, and looking for awesome projects that I can be a part of. I've recently designed two novels, a corporate website, a nonprofit's brand identity, and in my opinion, I'm doing the best work of my life. If you've got something that needs solutions found, ideas given form, stories discovered or just a bit of snark interjected into a situation, drop me a line and let's get started.
