My name's Steve Rokitka (aka Rocketkar) and I'm a full-stack product designer. What's that mean exactly? It means someone with the skills to look at a business need, research the problem space, then design, test and build solutions from prototype to production. Think of me as a designer who can code, or an engineer who can tell typefaces apart.

My career's always been a multidisciplinary one. I started out as a writer and illustrator, turned UI/UX designer, and rolled everything together with a liberal coating of usability research and developer training at Cornell University and Code Fellows Seattle. I've gone deepest on mobile apps, UI/pattern library creation and ecommerce design, along with some great indie publishing and brand/identity projects on the side.

When I'm not crafting various parts of the internet, I go on adventures that give me an excuse to learn new things. In the last couple years I've painted a house, rode a motorcycle to Burning Man, got Wilderness First Responder certified, learned to fix cars at a community college, and continued my training in various martial arts.

Today, I'm looking for awesome projects that I can be a part of. If you've got something that needs solutions found, ideas given form, stories discovered or just a bit of snark injected into a situation, drop me a line and let's get started.
