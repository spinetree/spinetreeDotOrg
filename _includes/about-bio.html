I'm Steve Rokitka (aka Steve Rocketkar). Product designer, UI/UX designer, design-usability-content unicorn - however you'd like to say it, I've been building tools and experiences for over a decade.

My career's always been a multidisciplinary one. I started out as a writer-editor, turned web designer, and rolled everything together with a liberal coating of usability research at Cornell University. I've gone deepest on mobile apps, search/catalog design and marketing site design, along with some great indie publishing, data visualization and brand/identity projects.

When I'm not crafting various parts of the internet, I go on all kinds of adventures that appeal to me. In the last couple years I've painted a house, rode a motorcycle to Burning Man, took a Wilderness First Responder course, learned to fix cars at a community college, and continued my training in various martial arts.

Today, I'm back at work full-time, and always looking for awesome projects that I can be a part of. If you've got something that needs solutions found, ideas given form, stories discovered or just a bit of snark injected into a situation, drop me a line and let's get started.
