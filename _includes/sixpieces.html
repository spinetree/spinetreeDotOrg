Six Pieces From My Professional Life

  • Be someone people can look forward to being around.
  • Everyone wants to feel listened to and respected. You can disagree with someone and still make them feel listened to and respected.
  • You can read someone's life story in the way they make a sandwich. The way you do anything is the way you do everything.
  • What someone wants and what they say they want are often different. Good design means reading between the lines.
  • People aren't using their logical brains as much as they think. They feel their way through most things. This is beautiful and terrifying and beautiful again.
  • Don't promise all the time. Just do what you say you're going to do.
